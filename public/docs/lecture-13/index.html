Lecture-13 - Social Class

1033 Program

We discussed the Clinton administration’s expansion of a pre-existing program to transfer military equipment to police departments, known as the “1033 Program”. The program has transferred billions in equipment to police departments, and has continued despite growing concern in the wake of the Ferguson protests and additional protests in Baltimore.
